# Traffic Friends Master Style Prompt v1.0

## Core Style

Create a cute toy-like 3D animation style for young children (ages 3–7).

### Character Design
- Large round eyes (about 35% of the face)
- Black pupils with two white highlights
- Always smiling
- Rounded body and soft edges
- Bright primary colors
- Smooth glossy plastic toy material
- Friendly and cute proportions
- Yellow five-point star emblem on every main character
- No scary faces
- No realistic textures

### Visual Style
- Bright daylight
- Clean colorful environment
- Child-friendly
- Pixar-quality 3D lighting
- Consistent character appearance across every image and video

### Animation Style
- Smooth movement
- Slow and easy to understand
- Fixed camera whenever possible
- Clear object movement
- Focus on vehicles and machines

### Shorts Formula
Mechanical movement
+
Realistic sound effects
+
Cute characters
+
One funny moment
=
30–60 second Shorts

Always keep every character identical in every episode.
