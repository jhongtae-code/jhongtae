# Kori (Train)

## Identity

Kori is the main character of Traffic Friends.

## Design Rules

- Cute toy-like 3D train
- Bright red body
- Rounded front shape
- Large round eyes (35% of face)
- Black pupils with two white highlights
- Large smiling mouth
- Yellow five-point star emblem on the front
- Smooth glossy plastic texture
- Rounded edges only
- Friendly expression
- No realistic train details
- Always looks identical in every episode

## Animation

- Slow smooth movement
- Gentle bouncing while moving
- Friendly eye blinking
- Happy expression

## Never Change

- Face shape
- Eye size
- Smile
- Body proportions
- Star emblem
- Main colors
